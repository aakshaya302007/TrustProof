About TrustScore
How TrustScore works

The problem we solve

190 million Indians have no credit score — not because they're untrustworthy, but because no one was listening. A farmer who pays electricity on time, a chai stall owner who recharges every month — she's reliable. TrustScore hears that.

Zero Knowledge Proof

Imagine someone asks "are you over 18?" You say yes. They don't need your birth certificate — just the answer. We do the same. The bank gets "score: 720, trustworthy: yes" — never your actual bills or phone records.

Why not just Aadhaar?

Aadhaar is great — but construction workers' fingerprints wear out. Some people never got it linked. TrustScore works with or without Aadhaar, using phone-based OTP identity instead.

Community trust layer

When a panchayat leader or teacher vouches for you digitally, that becomes a real signal. For people with zero digital footprint, community trust is the most powerful proof of all.
